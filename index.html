    Your questions answered simply
  


  

    Wondering how we can work together? Here are the answers to the most common questions I get from business owners — let's make your website journey easy and enjoyable.
  


  
  


    
    

      

        What do you specialise in?
      

      

        I create template-based websites for small and medium businesses, and transform designer materials into functional, clean, performant websites. I also provide ongoing hosting and maintenance services for businesses that want to focus on what they do best.
      

    


    
    

      

        Are you a website designer?
      

      

        My focus is web development, not creating complex designs. I can take any design or concept and turn it into a functional, high-performance website. For clients who need a faster solution, I offer template-based sites that are quick to launch while still looking professional.
      

    


    
    

      

        How fast can my site launch?
      

      

        Most standard projects wrap up in 1–2 weeks (up to 5 pages). More complex sites with custom integrations typically take 2–4 weeks. Communication is key — you’ll always know the progress of your project.
      

    


    
    

      

        Can you improve my current site?
      

      

        Absolutely! I can add new features, improve functionality, and tidy up your existing code. Depending on the technology used, sometimes rebuilding the site is faster and more efficient than upgrading the old one.
      

    


    
    

      

        Do you offer ongoing support?
      

      

        Yes! I provide flexible maintenance plans and am always available for updates, troubleshooting, or implementing new ideas. You’ll always have support when needed.
      

    


    
    

      

        How do we get started?
      

      

        Reach out with your ideas or requirements. We’ll discuss your goals, map out a plan, and start the project. I’ll guide you every step of the way.

My Journey

          

            My journey into frontend development started with curiosity about
            how websites work. After 13+ years in IT, helping organizations
            optimize datacenters and operations, I realized I wanted to
            understand how technology is experienced by end users.
          

          

            What began as tinkering with HTML and CSS has grown into a full
            skill set that allows me to build beautiful, responsive, and
            functional websites.
          

          

            I’m passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and designing
            interfaces that are intuitive and accessible. Whether using modern
            website builders or coding from scratch, I focus on simplicity and
            performance — no unnecessary “fluff”.
