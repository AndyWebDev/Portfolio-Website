BuiltByAndy Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

At BuiltByAndy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. When you provide your contact details via our website forms, we use your information solely to respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • We may collect personal information, such as your name, contact email, and contact telephone, when you contact us through our website.

2. Website Usage Information

  • We may collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, to improve our website's functionality and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

1. Providing Web Design and Web Development Services

  • We use your personal information to communicate with you about your project.

2. Communication

  • We may use your contact information to send appointment reminders, website updates, and other relevant information.

3. Improving Website Experience

  • Non-personal information is used to analyze trends, administer the website, and gather demographic information for aggregate use.

Information Sharing

1. Service Providers

  • We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as appointment scheduling and communication tools.

2. Legal Compliance

  • We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, respond to legal claims, or protect the rights, property, or safety of others.

Your Choices

1. Opt-Out

  • You may opt-out of receiving non-essential communications from us. However, certain communications related to your project may be necessary.

2. Access and Correction

  • You have the right to access and correct your personal information. Please contact us if you would like to review or update your information.

Consent

By submitting your personal information via our contact forms, you consent to our collection and use of your data as described in this policy. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us directly at contact@builtbyandy.com.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with our team to provide the requested services.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at contact@builtbyandy.com.
