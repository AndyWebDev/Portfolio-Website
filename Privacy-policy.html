This privacy notice tells you what to expect BuiltByAndy to do with your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use your data.

What Information We Collect, Use, and Why

We collect or use the following information to provide and improve products and services for clients:

  • Names and contact details
  • Usage data (including information about how you interact with and use our website, products, and services)

Lawful Bases and Data Protection Rights

Under UK data protection law, we must have a “lawful basis” for collecting and using your personal information. Our lawful basis is Consent—we have your permission after providing you with all relevant information. You may withdraw your consent at any time.

Your data protection rights include:

  • Right of access: Ask for copies of your personal information and details about how it’s used.
  • Right to rectification: Ask us to correct or delete inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to restriction of processing: Request we limit how we use your information.
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability: Ask that we transfer the data you provided to another organisation or to you.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time when consent is our lawful basis.

If you make a request, we will respond without undue delay and within one month. To exercise your rights, please contact us using the details provided above.

Where We Get Personal Information From

We collect personal information directly from you when you provide it to us.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep personal information for up to 1 year from the delivery of our services, after which it will be securely deleted.

How to Complain

If you have concerns about our use of your personal data, please contact us using the details at the top of this notice.
